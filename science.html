Science About Astronomy

The Latest Science From Astronomy

Orbiting the Earth since 1990, the Astronomy Space Telescope has helped to answer some of the most compelling astronomical questions of our time, and uncovered mysteries we never knew existed. With vision that spans the ultraviolet through visible and into the near infrared,Astronomy investigates everything from black holes to planets around other stars. Its unique capabilities are revolutionizing astronomy as continues humanity’s quest to explore the universe.
